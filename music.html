Publishing

As a music industry professional, I have focused my experiences on the publishing side of the business. In the past, I have interned with Sony/ATV Music Publishing, BMG Music Publishing, and I am currently working in the A&R and music publishing sides of Magnus Media, Marc Anthony's full service music company. I have a passion for developing creative opportunities, such as elevating songwriters' careers and nurturing their writing process, pitching for sync, and looking for new talent to collaborate with on various projects.
