Songwriting

I started writing songs in my bedroom when I was 14. At the time, it felt like I had truly stumbled into the closet thing I knew of to magic - creating something out of thin air that carries the power to affect and change people. To me, songwriting isn't a hobby or activity, it's a lifestyle that defines who I am. Throughout the years, I've continued to hone my craft through music camps, studying songwriting at the University of Miami, and spending my summers jumping into the co-writing circuits in Nashville.

Below is a set of demos of some of my favorite songs that I have recently written. I wrote some songs alone and am featured as the vocalist, and others I cowrote with different writers who are noted in the track title. I love working in a variety of genres, but my strongsuits are Pop, RnB, and Country/Folk.

Publishing

As a music industry professional, I have focused my experiences on the publishing side of the business. In the past, I have interned with Sony/ATV Music Publishing, BMG Music Publishing, and I am currently working in the AnR and music publishing sides of Magnus Media, Marc Anthony's full service music company. I have a passion for developing creative opportunities, such as elevating songwriters' careers and nurtuting their writing process, pitching for sync, and looking for new talent to collaborate with on various projects.

Management

This past October, I started managing Loren Cole. An impressive songstress with an honest voice, Loren Cole delivers clever melodies alongside lyrics that search for meaning through a heartfelt perspective. Based in Los Angeles, Loren is often compared to Missy Higgins or Patty Griffin, and her wide-array of influences range from the darker tones of Mat Kearney and Brandi Carlile, to the lighter music of Jack Johnson and Jason Mraz.

"Often," the lead single from her debut album, For The Sake Of Being Honest, will be released in January 2018!
